## 4. TAX STRUCTURE

### 4.1 Corporate Tax (9%)

**Key facts:**
- UAE corporate tax rate: **9% on taxable income above AED 375,000**
- Below AED 375,000: **0%**
- Registration with FTA: **MANDATORY within 3 months** of license issuance (AED 10,000 penalty if late)
- Tax returns: Filed within **9 months** of financial year end

**QFZP (Qualifying Free Zone Person) — 0% Tax**

Trip Tailors MAY qualify for 0% corporate tax on qualifying income if ALL conditions are met:

| Condition | Trip Tailors' Position |
|-----------|----------------------|
| Registered in a UAE Free Zone | Yes (IFZA) |
| Adequate substance in the Free Zone | Need to demonstrate: qualified employees, operating expenses, assets in FZ |
| Derives qualifying income | Service fees from international agents = likely qualifying (non-UAE income) |
| Non-qualifying income ≤ 5% of revenue or AED 5M | Need to monitor — UAE-based agents' fees may not qualify |
| Audited financial statements | Must prepare annually (cost: AED 5,000-10,000) |
| Arm's length transactions | Relevant if related party dealings |
| No election out of QFZP | Don't elect out |

**Realistic assessment:**
- In Year 1, revenue will likely be below AED 375,000 (~$102,000) → **0% tax regardless of QFZP**
- In Year 2+, if revenue exceeds AED 375,000, QFZP status matters
- Most of Trip Tailors' income comes from international agents (outside UAE) → likely qualifying
- **Budget for audit costs** (AED 5,000-10,000/year) to maintain QFZP eligibility
- Hire an accountant familiar with free zone corporate tax from Month 1

### 4.2 VAT (5%)

| Threshold | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Taxable supplies > AED 375,000/year | **Mandatory** VAT registration |
| Taxable supplies AED 187,500-375,000/year | **Voluntary** registration (recommended for input tax recovery) |
| Below AED 187,500 | No registration needed |

**Trip Tailors' VAT position:**
- Services to agents OUTSIDE UAE: **Zero-rated** (0% VAT, but you can still claim input VAT)
- Services to agents INSIDE UAE: **5% VAT applies**
- Year 1: Likely below mandatory threshold → register voluntarily to recover VAT on Dubai expenses (rent, legal, services)
- Quarterly VAT returns required once registered

### 4.3 Personal Income Tax
**ZERO.** No personal income tax in the UAE. Your founder salary is tax-free.

### 4.4 Tax Comparison: UK vs Dubai

| Tax Type | UK (Current) | Dubai |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| Corporate tax | 25% (on profits > £250,000) | 0-9% (QFZP: 0% on qualifying income) |
| Personal income tax | 20-45% (on salary) | 0% |
| VAT | 20% | 5% |
| Capital gains | 20% (on company sale) | 0% |
| Dividend tax | 8.75-39.35% | 0% |

**Estimated annual tax savings by operating from Dubai (at $150K revenue):**
- UK tax on $150K revenue (rough): $25,000-$40,000 (corporate + personal)
- Dubai tax: ~$0 (below threshold or QFZP)
- **Annual saving: $25,000-$40,000**
