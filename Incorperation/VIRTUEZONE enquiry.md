Subject: IFZA FZE Setup — Technology — Quote Request

Dear Virtuzone Team,

I'm a UK national planning to incorporate a technology company in IFZA. I'd like to request an exact quote for the following setup:

**Company Details:**
- Structure: FZE (Free Zone Establishment) — single shareholder
- Free Zone: IFZA
- License Type: Commercial
- Activities (3): Technology Services, Consultancy
- Company Name: Trip Tailors FZE (preferred) — please advise on name availability
- Visas: 1 investor visa (for founder), 2nd for my girlfriend
- Office: Flexi desk (minimum required)

**Please provide an all-inclusive quote covering:**

1. Trade license fee
2. Certificate of incorporation
3. Memorandum of Association
4. Share certificate
5. Establishment card
6. E-channel registration
7. Flexi desk (1 year)
8. Investor visa processing (entry permit, status change, stamping)
9. Medical fitness test appointment
10. Emirates ID processing
11. Your service/consultant fee

**Additional questions:**

1. What is the EXACT total cost for everything listed above, with zero hidden fees? Please confirm what IS and IS NOT included.

2. Do you handle FTA corporate tax registration (CTRN) as part of the package? If not, what is the additional cost?

3. Which banks do you provide introductions to? Is there a partnership with Mashreq NeoBiz? Can you guarantee account opening, or is it best-effort?

4. What is the complete timeline from signing your agreement to having the visa stamped in my passport?

5. I will need a basic health insurance policy for the visa. Do you arrange this, and what is the cost?

6. What are the annual renewal costs through Virtuzone (license + visa + establishment card + e-channel)?

7. Do you offer multi-year packages (2, 3, or 5 years)? What are the discounts?

8. Is UBO (Ultimate Beneficial Owner) filing included in your service?

9. Do you provide ongoing compliance support (renewal reminders, FTA filing support)?

10. I have an existing UK company (Trip Tailors Travel Ltd) that I plan to make dormant. Do you have any advisory services for this, or is this separate?

**About the business:**
Trip Tailors is a technology platform that provides SaaS tools, lead generation, and branding services to independent travel agents worldwide. We do NOT sell travel directly — we are a technology and service provider. Our revenue comes from agent subscriptions and service fee commission splits processed via Stripe.

I'm targeting incorporation within the next 8 weeks and plan to relocate from the UK to Dubai.

Thank you for your time. I look forward to your detailed quote.

Best regards,
Calum O'Gorman

## What to Expect from Their Response

They will likely:
1. Send a quote within 24-48 hours
2. Offer a free consultation call
3. Try to upsell add-ons (PRO services, premium office, extra visas)
4. Provide a timeline estimate

## Red Flags to Watch For
- Vague pricing ("starting from..." without specifics)
- Establishment card or e-channel listed as "extra" when it should be included
- Pressure to upgrade office tier
- No clear answer on what's NOT included
