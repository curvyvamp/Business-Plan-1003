## 1. FREE ZONE DECISION: IFZA vs DSO vs DMCC

### Side-by-Side Comparison (Verified January 2026 Pricing)

| Factor | IFZA | DSO | DMCC |
|--------|------|-----|------|
| **License + 1 Visa (Year 1)** | AED 14,900 (~$4,050) | AED 12,000 license + AED 28,392 mandatory office = AED 40,392 (~$11,000) | AED 20,000+ (~$5,450+) |
| **License + 1 Visa (all-in with visa processing)** | AED 18,900-23,150 (~$5,150-$6,300) | AED 45,252 (~$12,300) | AED 30,000+ (~$8,170+) |
| **Office Requirement** | Virtual/flexi desk INCLUDED in package | MANDATORY physical office (minimum 13.5 sqm = AED 28,392/yr) | Flexi desk available but separate fee |
| **Annual Renewal** | AED 10,000-15,000 (~$2,720-$4,080) | AED 10,000-12,000 + office renewal (~$5,450+) | AED 20,000-35,000+ (~$5,450-$9,530) |
| **Visa Type** | Investor/partner visa (2-3 years) | Manager visa (2 years, NO investor visa) | Investor visa (2-3 years) |
| **Visa Flexibility** | Start with 0, add up to 6 | Limited by office size | Tied to office tier |
| **Setup Speed** | 2-5 business days | 5-7 business days | 5-10 business days |
| **Activities Included** | Up to 3 activities in package | Usually 1 activity | 1 activity |
| **Banking Ease** | Standard (Mashreq partnership) | Good (established zone) | Best (JLT location, bank proximity) |
| **Tech Credibility** | General (not tech-specific) | High (Silicon Oasis = tech brand) | Medium (commodities/trading reputation) |
| **Audit Requirement** | Not mandatory for small entities (unless QFZP) | Similar to IFZA | Mandatory annually |
| **Multi-Year Discount** | 15% off 2yr, 20% off 3yr, 30% off 5yr | Limited discounts | Some multi-year options |
| **Share Capital** | AED 1,000 minimum | AED 1,000 minimum | AED 50,000 minimum |

### VERDICT: IFZA (Clear Winner)

**Why IFZA is the right choice for Trip Tailors:**

1. **Cost:** ~AED 18,900 all-in for Year 1 (license + 1 visa) vs AED 45,000+ for DSO. **Saves ~AED 26,000 ($7,000) in Year 1 alone.**
2. **No mandatory office lease:** Flexi desk/virtual office included. DSO requires a physical office (AED 28,392/yr) you don't need as a solo digital founder.
3. **Investor visa available:** DSO only offers "Manager" visas, not investor visas. For fundraising optics, "Investor Visa" looks better to Dubai angel investors.
4. **Multi-year savings:** 3-year package saves 20%, locking in rates and avoiding annual price changes.
5. **3 activities included:** Register "Technology Services," "E-Commerce," and "Consultancy" under one license — covers all Trip Tailors' operations.
6. **Faster setup:** 2-5 days vs 5-10 for others.

**The DSO "tech credibility" argument is weak because:**
- Your product, traction, and pitch speak louder than your free zone name
- Most investors won't know or care which free zone you're in
- You can relocate to DSO later if it becomes strategically important (free zone transfers are possible)
- Saving $7,000 in Year 1 preserves runway for actual business operations
