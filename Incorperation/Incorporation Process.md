## 3. STEP-BY-STEP INCORPORATION PROCESS

### Week-by-Week Timeline

---

### PRE-DEPARTURE (Weeks 1-2, From UK)

**Week 1: Preparation**

| Day | Action | Details | Cost |
|-----|--------|---------|------|
| Mon | Decision: Confirm IFZA | Email IFZA or a setup consultant for exact current pricing | Free |
| Mon | Choose business activities | "Technology Services" + "E-Commerce" + "Consultancy" | Included |
| Tue | Choose company structure | Free Zone Establishment (FZE) — single shareholder (you) | Standard |
| Tue | Reserve company name | "Trip Tailors FZE" — submit 3 options to IFZA | Usually free |
| Wed | Prepare documents pack | See checklist below | Your time |
| Thu | Submit IFZA application online | Via IFZA portal or setup agent | Application fee included in package |
| Fri | Pay initial license fees | Wire transfer or card | AED 14,900 |

**Documents to prepare (from UK):**
- [ ] Passport copy (scan, colour, 6+ months validity)
- [ ] Passport-sized photos (white background, digital format)
- [ ] UK proof of address (utility bill or bank statement, less than 3 months old)
- [ ] CV/resume (1 page, highlighting business experience)
- [ ] Business plan summary (1-2 pages — use the Executive Summary from the Master Business Plan)
- [ ] Bank reference letter (from your UK bank — request this NOW, takes 5-10 business days)
- [ ] No criminal record self-declaration (IFZA provides template)
- [ ] UK Good Conduct Certificate (police check — apply via ACRO, takes 10-15 business days)

**CRITICAL: Order the ACRO certificate NOW.** It takes the longest and is often required for visa processing. Apply at acro.police.uk. Cost: £55.

**Week 2: Approval + Travel Prep**

| Day | Action | Details |
|-----|--------|---------|
| Mon-Wed | IFZA reviews application | Typically 1-3 business days |
| Wed | Receive approval + MOA | Sign Memorandum of Association digitally |
| Thu | Pay remaining package fees | Visa allocation, establishment card, e-channel |
| Thu | Book flights to Dubai | One-way (you're relocating) |
| Fri | Arrange temporary accommodation | Airbnb or hotel for first 2-4 weeks |

**Accommodation budget (first month):** AED 3,000-6,000 ($820-$1,630) for studio Airbnb in Business Bay/JLT/Downtown area.

### POST-SETUP (Weeks 5-8)

**Week 5-6: Legal + Platform**
- Legal counsel drafts Agent IC Agreement
- Legal counsel adapts Referrer Agreement for Dubai
- Terms of Service and Privacy Policy drafted
- Review and iterate on all contracts
- Begin agent platform development sprint (MongoDB migration, auth, Stripe)

**Week 7-8: Operational Readiness**
- Contracts finalized and signed off by legal
- Platform development continues
- Accounting setup (Xero or Zoho Books)
- First referrer contract re-signed under Dubai entity
- Agent recruitment marketing prepared
- RateHawk account fully set up with test bookings
- Training content creation begins

**TOTAL TIME: License issued to fully operational = 6-8 weeks**
