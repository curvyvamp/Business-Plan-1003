### Call Script for Agent Demos

```
INTRO (1 min)
"Thanks for taking the time, [Name]. I'll keep this brief — 15 minutes 
max. I'd love to show you what we're building and get your honest 
feedback. Sound good?"

THEIR BACKGROUND (2 min)
"Before I dive in — tell me about your business. How long have you 
been an agent? What's your specialty? What tools do you currently use?"

THE PROBLEM (2 min)
"So most agents I talk to struggle with three things:
1. Finding quality leads without spending a fortune
2. Juggling multiple tools that don't talk to each other
3. Looking professional without a big brand behind them
Does that resonate with your experience?"

THE SOLUTION (5 min)
"That's exactly what Trip Tailors solves. Let me show you..."
[DEMO: Dashboard → Email Builder → Lead Marketplace concept]

THE OFFER (2 min)
"We're looking for 10-15 founding agents to shape the platform. 
As a founding agent, you'd get:
• Free 3-month trial (normally $299 signup + $45/month)
• Your feedback directly shapes the features
• First access to leads from our 50M weekly TikTok views
• Locked-in preferred commission tier

All I'm asking for right now is a Letter of Intent — non-binding, 
just says you're interested in joining when we launch."

CLOSE (2 min)
"Based on what you've seen, is this something you'd want to be part of?"

[If yes] "Great! I'll send over the LOI today. Any questions before we wrap?"
[If maybe] "What would you need to see to feel confident about this?"
[If no] "No problem. Would you mind sharing what's holding you back? 
Your feedback helps us improve."
```
