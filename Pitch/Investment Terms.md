## 5. INVESTMENT TERMS — RECOMMENDED

### Instrument: SAFE Note (Simple Agreement for Future Equity)
**Why SAFE over equity round:**
- Standard for pre-seed globally
- Simpler legal process (lower legal costs)
- No valuation negotiation required upfront
- Converts to equity at next priced round (seed)

**Recommended Terms:**
| Term | Value | Rationale |
|------|-------|-----------|
| Investment amount | $275,000-$325,000 | 12-month runway with buffer |
| Valuation cap | $2,000,000-$2,500,000 | Conservative for pre-seed with significant traction |
| Discount | 20% | Standard SAFE discount |
| Pro-rata rights | Yes | Gives investors right to maintain ownership in future rounds |
| MFN (Most Favored Nation) | Yes | Standard protection |

**At $300K raised on $2.5M cap:**
- Effective dilution at conversion: ~12%
- Founder retains: ~88% (pre-conversion)
- Investor gets: ~12% (on conversion at seed round)

**Why $2M-$2.5M cap (not higher):**
- Realistic for pre-seed with no revenue yet
- Demonstrates founder humility and alignment with investors
- Creates upside for early investors when seed round values at $5M-$10M
- Avoids the "down round" risk if valuation cap is too aggressive

### Alternative: Equity Round
If investors prefer equity:
| Term | Value |
|------|-------|
| Pre-money valuation | $1.5M-$2M |
| Investment | $275K-$325K |
| Post-money valuation | $1.775M-$2.325M |
| Investor ownership | 14-18% |
| Founder ownership | 82-86% |
