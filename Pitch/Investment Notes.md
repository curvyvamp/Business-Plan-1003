## 1. INVESTMENT LANDSCAPE — DUBAI/UAE (2025-2026)

### Market Context
- UAE startup ecosystem: 3,500+ startups, growing rapidly
- Total VC investment in UAE: ~$2.5B annually (2025)
- Pre-seed/seed rounds: $500K-$2M average
- Travel tech: Selective but growing interest post-pandemic
- Dubai government actively supports startups via free zone incentives, accelerators, and visa programs

### Why Dubai for Fundraising
1. **Proximity to capital** — Dubai has a high concentration of HNWIs and family offices
2. **Travel industry hub** — Emirates, Etihad, major hotel chains headquartered here
3. **Tax efficiency** — Investors appreciate 0% capital gains on exits
4. **Government support** — Programs like Dubai Future Foundation, DET innovation initiatives
5. **Global reach** — GCC investors often co-invest with European/Asian funds

## 3. RECOMMENDED APPROACH — PHASED STRATEGY

### Phase 1: Accelerator Applications (Weeks 1-4)
**Priority: Intelak Hub**
- Apply to Intelak's next cohort immediately
- AED 50,000 (~$13,600) non-dilutive grant
- Mentorship from Emirates, Amadeus, Dubai Airports
- Demo day exposure to travel industry investors
- Even if not accepted: the application process sharpens your pitch

**Also apply to:**
- Plug and Play (DET partnership)
- Flat6Labs (if Intelak timeline doesn't align)

### Phase 2: Pitch Deck & Materials (Weeks 2-6)
- Finalize pitch deck (10-12 slides)
- Build financial model spreadsheet (interactive)
- Prepare demo video of agent platform + referral hub
- Draft executive summary (1-pager) for cold outreach
- Create data room (Google Drive): pitch deck, financials, contracts, product screenshots, traction proof

### Phase 3: Angel Network Outreach (Weeks 6-10)
- Submit to Dubai Angel Investors
- Attend Dubai startup events (Dubai Startup Hub events, STEP Conference if timing aligns)
- LinkedIn direct outreach to 20-30 angels with travel/tech background
- Request warm introductions through any existing network connections

### Phase 4: Individual Angel Meetings (Weeks 8-14)
- Target 15-20 pitch meetings
- Aim for 3-5 angels contributing $50K-$100K each
- Or 1-2 larger investors at $150K-$275K

### Phase 5: Close & Legal (Weeks 12-16)
- Negotiate terms (SAFE note recommended)
- Legal review of investment documents
- Funds received; begin execution

### Total Timeline: 12-16 weeks from start to close
