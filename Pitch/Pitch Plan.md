## 4. PITCH DECK STRUCTURE (10 SLIDES)

### Slide 1: Title & Hook (30 seconds)
**Trip Tailors — Travel, Tailored to You**
"We're building the operating system for independent travel agents — combining AI-powered tools, social media lead generation, and professional branding to help agents earn more while staying independent."

Key stat: "50 million weekly TikTok views already driving travel leads to our platform."

### Slide 2: Problem (1.5 minutes)
**Three interconnected problems:**
1. Independent agents lack affordable, integrated tools and quality leads
2. Consumers can't easily find trustworthy, personalized travel agents
3. Travel influencers have no structured way to monetize their audiences for bespoke travel

Visual: Show the fragmented landscape — agents juggling 5+ separate tools, paying $200-$500/month

### Slide 3: Solution (2 minutes)
**Trip Tailors connects all three through one platform:**
- Left: Agent Dashboard (screenshot) — CRM, email builder, invoice gen, lead marketplace
- Center: Public Website — lead capture form for consumers
- Right: Referral Hub (screenshot) — influencer dashboard, training, payments

"Agents keep their independence. We provide the brand, tools, and leads."

### Slide 4: Product Demo (1.5 minutes)
**Live demo or video showing:**
1. Agent creating a professional email itinerary (30 seconds)
2. Lead appearing in the marketplace from a referrer (15 seconds)
3. Referrer dashboard showing earnings (15 seconds)
4. Client receiving a branded itinerary via portal link (15 seconds)

### Slide 5: Business Model (1 minute)
**Four revenue streams:**
| Stream | Amount |
|--------|--------|

| Monthly subscription | $45/month |
| Commission split | 80/20 (agent/TT) |
| Lead marketplace | $5-$20/lead |

"Each agent generates ~$6,000/year in revenue. Our target: 100 agents = $600K ARR."

- Referral partner contract executed and tested

- **Trial (Months 4-6):** 10-20 agents onboarded free to validate and iterate
